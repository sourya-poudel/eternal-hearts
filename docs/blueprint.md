# **App Name**: Eternal Hearts

## Core Features:

- Photo Gallery: Interactive photo gallery with albums or timeline view, smooth transitions, and soft animations. Organized chronologically, starting with photos from December 16, 2023.
- Video Memories: Clean, scrollable grid or carousel to showcase videos with captions and ambient effects.
- Love Letters: A dreamy letters and confessions section with hand-written-style fonts and animated heart touches for interactive love notes.
- Audio Messages: Audio messages space where voice recordings or music dedicated to each other can be uploaded and played with a themed audio player.
- Timeline Highlights: Sweet timeline feature marking key dates: First Meeting (December 16, 2023) and Anniversary (April 1, 2025).

- Accent color: Muted Rose (#D8BFD8) for highlighting important elements, providing subtle contrast.
- Headline font: 'Belleza', a humanist sans-serif for an elegant, romantic feel. Body font: 'Alegreya', a humanist serif for a readable, gentle feel.
